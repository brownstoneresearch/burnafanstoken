$BURNA Project Roadmap

The $BURNA roadmap is built to evolve in four strategic phases, delivering community value from token launch through to ecosystem expansion, creator infrastructure, and global cultural impact.

Phase 1: Launch & Foundation (Q2 2025)

  • Token Creation & Audit — Deployed and verified on Ethereum, with audited smart contracts
  • Website & Whitepaper Release — Public transparency through launch site
  • Community Airdrops & Whitelisting — Rewards for early adopters and Afrobeat ambassadors
  • DEX Listing (Uniswap) — Liquidity provision and token analytics dashboard
  • Staking Pool Live — Stake $BURNA for passive rewards

Phase 2: Ecosystem Growth (Q3 2025)

  • NFT Genesis Drop — Afrobeat-themed NFT collection (art, music, perks)
  • Creator Onboarding Program — Digital artists and musicians join the platform
  • DAO Governance Activation — Snapshot voting and proposal system launched
  • Merch Store (Beta) — Apparel and NFT-redeemable merch for holders
  • Analytics & Wallet Dashboard — View staking, balances, votes, and more

Phase 3: Utility & Integration (Q4 2025)

  • DeFi Expansion — Yield farming, DAO-managed burns, and liquidity rewards
  • NFT Ticketing System — Access to concerts and private events
  • Cross-Chain Deployment — Bridges to L2s like Base, BNB Chain
  • Buyback & Burn Mechanism — Revenue-powered token reduction
  • Creator Toolkits — Tools for minting, proposal creation, and campaigns

Phase 4: Global Expansion (2026+)

  • CEX Listings — Gate.io, OKX, KuCoin and more
  • Launch of the "BurnaVerse" — Virtual concerts, galleries, NFT hubs
  • Afrobeat Web3 Summit — IRL & virtual creator events worldwide
  • $BURNA Academy — Education partnerships with African institutions
  • Global Ambassadors & City DAOs — Community-driven meetups and hackathons
