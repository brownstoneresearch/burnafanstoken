Decentralized Autonomous Organization ($BURNA DAO)

The $BURNA ecosystem is governed by its community of token holders through a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). This governance structure ensures decisions are made transparently and in the best interest of Afrobeat culture and its global supporters.

What is the $BURNA DAO?

A DAO is a smart contract-powered system enabling $BURNA holders to:

  • Propose initiatives
  • Vote on critical decisions
  • Allocate treasury resources
  • Shape the future of the ecosystem â€” all on-chain

Who Can Participate?

  • All $BURNA holders are eligible to vote
  • A minimum of 10,000 tokens may be required to propose (DAO-defined)
  • Staked tokens may carry more weight

Types of Governance Proposals

  • Platform Upgrades: Add new features or optimize contracts
  • Treasury Allocation: Fund artist grants or development initiatives
  • Tokenomics Adjustments: Modify rewards or burn strategies
  • NFT or Merch Initiatives: Approve collections and partnerships
  • DAO Expansion: Launch City DAOs, onboard ambassadors

Treasury Management

The DAO controls a transparent treasury funded by token reserves, fees, and staking pools. Funds are only spent via approved proposals and multisig execution.

Governance Tools

  • Snapshot: Gasless voting
  • Gnosis Safe: Multisig wallet for security
  • Governance Portal: Dashboard for proposal submissions and votes

Participation Rewards

  • Earn NFT badges and incentives
  • Eligible for DAO ambassador roles or proposal leads
  • Exclusive access to drops, staking boosts, and perks
